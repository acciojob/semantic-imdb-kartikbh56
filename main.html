Summary

*Inception* is a mind-bending science fiction film that follows a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who specializes in stealing secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform inception — planting an idea into someone’s mind.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
